Welcome to the Crystal Guessing Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score
You win the game by matching your total score to the bumber displayed below. If you go over the number, you lose.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will reset to a different number, as will the target number.
